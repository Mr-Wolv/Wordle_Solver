HOW TO PLAY

  1. Type your guess. Just press the letter keys — no box to click. The word appears on the board's active row (max 5 letters). Or click any word in the POOL card to load it.
  2. Fix a tile's color. After you type, click each tile on the active row to cycle it absent → present → correct — matching the colors from your real Wordle.
  3. Submit. Press Enter or the on-screen ENTER key. The board's SOLVE list is your optimal next word — but you're free to play any word from POOL; a different pick may even solve faster.
  4. Delete. Press Backspace or the on-screen DELETE key to remove the last letter typed.
  5. NYT hint (optional). Type one consonant + one vowel in the hint box and log them. It's an official Wordle mechanic — not required. The solver already closes 100% with no hints; hints can shave a turn.
  6. Mode & lock. Toggle HARD MODE any time before your first guess. Once you submit turn 1, the mode + hints lock (you'll see a "DOMAIN LOCKED" notice) for the rest of the game.
  7. Done? Use SYSTEM RESET for a new puzzle. EXIT APP quits.
